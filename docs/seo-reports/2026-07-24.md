## Five highest-impact fixes

1. **Deploy the accumulated automation work.** Six researched pages and the
   corrected `www` URL helpers remain absent from the live site and sitemap.
2. **Remove trust and conversion blockers.** Enable the contact form, replace
   pricing placeholder copy, and reconcile the live PPF service with the
   `/about` statement that PPF is still in development.
3. **Improve mobile delivery and sharing.** Reduce the 4.6s mobile LCP and
   provide the missing Open Graph image instead of advertising a 404 asset.
4. **Replace the short shared town-page structure.** Add useful, genuinely
   local decisions and evidence rather than expanding schema around thin copy.
5. **Strengthen verifiable local entity signals.** Align a confirmed Google
   Business Profile, public phone/address or service-area details, opening
   hours, and profile links before adding them to LocalBusiness schema.
