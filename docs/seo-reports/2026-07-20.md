# RIGN daily SEO audit — 2026-07-20

## Score: 66/100

This is the first report in the daily series. The score describes the live
site before today's new Sheffield chrome-delete page is deployed, so future
reports have an honest baseline.

No service × town pages were live at audit time. |

## Five highest-impact fixes

1. **Build genuinely distinct service × town pages.** Use local research and
   different section orders, then link each from both its service pillar and
   town hub. This improves local depth and directly tackles the highest-risk
   uniqueness category.
2. **Reduce mobile LCP.** The live homepage's simulated mobile LCP was 4.7s
   despite an otherwise strong Lighthouse result. Review the responsive hero
   image payload and above-the-fold loading before changing lower-impact code.
3. **Deepen the guides hub.** Expand the current short articles with
   answer-first sections, practical decision criteria, source-backed local
   detail, and visible reviewed/updated dates.
4. **Strengthen local structured data and FAQs.** Add Service, FAQPage,
   LocalBusiness, and BreadcrumbList together on combination pages; add
   genuinely useful local FAQs where town pages can support them.
5. **Make freshness signals precise.** Store real per-route modification dates
   instead of assigning every sitemap entry the current build time, and only
   change reader-facing “updated” dates when content materially changes.
