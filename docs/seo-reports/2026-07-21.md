## Five highest-impact fixes

1. **Deploy and cross-link the distinct queued pages.** The two service × town
   pages and today's guide already pass local QA but are not present in the live
   sitemap, so none can yet earn impressions or strengthen topical depth.
2. **Reduce homepage mobile LCP.** The repeat mobile lab run measured 4.84s
   while desktop remained below one second. Audit the responsive hero image and
   above-the-fold slideshow payload before lower-impact performance changes.
3. **Rewrite and deepen the five town pages.** Their short shared structure is
   still the site's largest duplication risk. Add town-specific decision help
   and internal links only where research supports genuinely different copy.
4. **Expand useful FAQ and Service schema coverage.** Town and general pages
   should gain schema only alongside visible, page-specific answers; avoid
   adding boilerplate solely to increase the schema count.
5. **Tighten remaining on-page signals.** Review the two out-of-band meta
   descriptions, classify the four empty homepage alts correctly, and replace
   build-time sitemap dates with honest route-level modification dates.
